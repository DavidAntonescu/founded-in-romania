About Founded in Romania

Romania it’s one of the fewest countries in the world that still preserve old traditions. The Romanian folklore is probably the most varied and traditional in the whole of Europe.

But, Romania it’s not only about traditions and ancient history, it has long been acknowledged as one of the best destinations for IT companies, due to the large and talented pool of IT graduates and professionals.

Founded in Romania it’s about praising the Romanian ingenuity in the 21st century.

Inspired by Founded in Holland, we’ve composed a list of truly innovative Romanian startups. What do all these companies have in common?

  1. They were founded in Romania in the 21st century
  2. They have the ambition to transform their industry
  3. They promise to try to make it possible to download beer

Do you want to support this initiative? You can submit a Romanian startup that meets these criteria, download the press kit, or follow us on Facebook and Twitter to get notifications about new additions.
